About Malaysia

Malaysia is a country blessed with beautiful natural beaches and islands, as she is made up of a large peninsular and the third largest island in the world. As Malaysia has a long history of civilisation and colonialisation, you can find traces of Portuguese, Dutch, English and Japanese architectural and cultural heritage in Malaysia. You must also visit Malaysia if you want to taste different kinds of oriental cuisine because here in Malaysia we have Malay, Chinese, Indian and a lot of other types of cuisines. Malaysia is also a must-visit place for those who want see different culture and religious practices in a harmonious community. We have some of the most beautiful mosques, largest Hindu temple complex and several gigantic Buddha monuments.
