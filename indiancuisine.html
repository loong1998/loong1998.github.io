Malaysian Indian cuisine is basically founded on banana leaves, Chettinad food and Mamal culture. It implies the MalayIndian mix is started from South India, with fixings that can likewise be found in Malaysia and intersely spiced cooking techniques.

Banana Leaf Rice

In Malaysia, the term of "Banana Leaf Rice" is utilized to depict a well known dish, which is basically an Indian thali. Initially from South India, the dish comprises of a hill aof plain rice, with three or four distinctive vegetable dishes served on a banana leaf slice to fit the glorious spread.

The dish is improved with chutnets, pickles and the diner's desicion of fish, chicken and even crab curry poured over the white rice, while vegeterians have a decision of spinach or onion curries just as dhall, a chickpea based gravy.

Banana leaf rice would no be complete without the crunchy pappadom, which resemble huge crisps made out of lentil, chickpea and gram flour just as rice flour.

Roti Canai

In the word of Roti Canai, Roti alludes to an unleavened flatbread eaten in India, other South Asian nations and in certain parts of the Caribbean and Africa. There are numerous assortments in any cases, for reasons for separating the terms "roti" and "roti canai", we focus on the North Indian paratha roti. To make paratha, bread dough is coated in ghee (clarified butter), rolled very thin, sprinkled with more ghee, folded like a paper fan, folded into a curl, rolled meagerly at that point cooked in an oiled frying pan called tawa.

Indian travelers carried their cooking with them and affected the foods of their received nations. In Malaysia, it became Roti Canai.

Nasi Biryani

Nasi Biryani is an incredibly famous Indian dish, it requeires time and practice to make however it worth every bit if the exertion. Long-grained rice flavored with fragrant flavors like saffron and layered with lamb, chicken, fish or vegetables and a thick gravy. The dish is then covered, its top got with batter and afterward the biryani is cooked over a low fire.
